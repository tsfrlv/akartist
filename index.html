AK.artist

AK.artist is Andris Kačanovskis. He was born in 1975 on 27th of June. His hometown is Riga, a capital of small country Latvia.

Andris Kačanovskis

AK mākslinieks in english translates as Oh, Ah or O artist. He started his dance career in 1993 when he proffesionally started to dance scenic folk dance in ensembles Dardedze, Daile and Dancis. AK artist also worked in Latvian opera and ballet house. But his true love is contemporary dance. He worked for many years in Olga Žitluhina dance company and also us a freelance dancer and choreographer in different projects. Now he also enjoys Tango and Areal dance. AK artist like wine only with years becomes better,bitter and better.
